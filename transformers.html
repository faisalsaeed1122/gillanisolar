Description

PEMPAK has state of the art transformer manufacturing facility, equipped with the latest technology and innovative equipment, enabling the production of high-quality transformers that meet the most stringent industry standards.

PEMPAK specializes in manufacturing a comprehensive range of transformers, including:
  • Power Transformers: Designed to handle high-voltage transmission and distribution, these transformers play a critical role in ensuring reliable and efficient power supply.
  • Distribution Transformers: Engineered to meet the specific needs of distribution networks, these transformers facilitate the safe and efficient delivery of electricity to end-users.
Transformer Types PEMPAK offers two primary types of transformers:  
  • Oil-Type Transformers: Utilizing oil as a cooling and insulating medium, these transformers are renowned for their reliability and durability.
  • Dry-Type Transformers: Employing air or gas as a cooling medium, these transformers are ideal for applications requiring reduced maintenance and increased safety.
  • Range 50KVA TO 4000KVA
